About Me

I'm C.J. Adeszko - an Illinois transplant living in Scottsdale, AZ. Creativity and passion are probably two of my most prominent character traits.

After working in the advertising industry for a couple of years, I decided to make a career change and become a developer.

I participated in Bloc's Software Developer curriculum online while working full-time and learned the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Ruby, Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, and Computer Science principles such as data structures, ORM databases, and searching and sorting algorithms.

When I'm coding, I focus on creating a maintainable codebase that follows convention and presents a clean design to users. I'm still learning every day and I welcome the opportunity to work with more experienced developers as it provides the potential for personal and professional growth.

When I'm not working to hone my skills as a developer, you're likely to find me playing my guitar and recording music, driving aimlessly through the mountains, or gaming on Xbox.
